About Gayatri Jewellers

25 Years of Unwavering Trust

Founded on the principles of purity and transparency, Gayatri Jewellers has been Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar's most trusted family jeweller for over two decades. We believe jewellery is more than an adornment—it is a legacy passed through generations.

Specializing in 100% Hallmarked Gold, we craft exquisite pieces for every occasion, blending traditional Maharashtrian artistry with contemporary elegance.
